How does Landsat work?

  • More than just a camera with a great zoom lens orbiting Earth
  • Not actually intended just to take images.. but collect quantitative data for analysis
  • Landsat measures light reflected from the earth's surface
  • By combining bands of light, you can create useful images

How Landsat satellites collect data

Each Landsat scene has 11 bands

Bands 4,3 & 2 are those needed to make a true colour image
